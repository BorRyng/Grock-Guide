About Grock

Grock is a unique tank/fighter in Mobile Legends, known for his stone-like durability, wall-creating abilities, and high burst damage in certain builds. Whether you play him as a roam tank, an EXP laner, or even jungle, Grock’s early aggression and map control make him a deadly presence on the battlefield.

Playstyles & Strategies

  • Wall Blocking: Cut off enemy paths and trap squishies.
  • Bush Control: Maximize Grock's passive for extra defense and damage.
  • Flicker + Ult: Surprise initiation combo to displace the enemy.
  • Early Invasion: Grock is great at stealing buffs or disturbing enemy junglers.
  • Split Push: Grock can clear waves quickly and tower dive safely.

Countering Grock

  • Pick heroes with mobility (e.g., Chou, Lancelot) or knock-ups (e.g., Kaja, Akai).
  • Use magic damage burst heroes to counter his high physical resistance.
  • Avoid grouping up near walls—Grock can punish with his ult.
